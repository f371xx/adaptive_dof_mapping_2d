2D Adaptive Simulation - Controls

You can either use your keyboard keys or a gamepad. For gamepad, see Image below.
Adaptive Controls are only available on keys.

Effect Keys
Move forwards UP-key or I
Move backwards DOWN-key or K
Move left LEFT-key or J
Move right RIGHT-key or L
Rotate left CTRL or U
Rotate right 0 or O
Close Gripper 3 or M
Open Gripper 1 or N
Cancel Simulation Escape
Restart Simulation R
Output State P
Toggle Adaptive HUD H
Adaptive Control DoF 1 W (+) and S (-)
Adaptive Control DoF 2 D (+) and A (-)
Adaptive Control: Next DoF E
Adaptive Control: Reset DoFs Q

Thank you for traveling with Adaptive Control!
